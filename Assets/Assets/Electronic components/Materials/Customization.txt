You can adjust albedo color for next materials:
-EC_Box(Every type of)
-EC_Inductors
-EC_Wires
Just click on need-able material,then right of 'albedo' slot you will see white box,click on it and choose the color you need.

You can adjust emission color for next materials:
-EC_Diodes
Just click on need-able material,then right of 'Emission' slot you will see white box,click on it and choose the color you need.

You can adjust resistor marking by putting "Electronic components/Materials/Textures_editable/Resistors_Detail_Marking" map into "Detail Albedo x2" slot of next materials:
-EC_Resistors_Blue
-EC_Resistors_Brown
-EC_Resistors_Green
